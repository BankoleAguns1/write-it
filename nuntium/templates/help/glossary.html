moderation

If moderation is enabled, your site will not send messages until you have approved them.

testing mode

A WriteIt site in testing mode will not send messages to the email addresses in its database — instead it will send them to the site's owner. Also WriteIt, doesn't publicise testing sites: whenever WriteIt dispolays a list of sites, testing sites are not shown (unless you're logged in as the sites' owner).
